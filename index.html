About Me

I am an Industrial Design student studying at the University of Canberra completing my degree with a breadth major in Interaction Design in the first half of 2023. I am interested in elegant designs and designing for everyone and am inspired by new technology and technology from modern history as well as biomimicry and organic design. I am excited to begin applying myself in the design industry.

A7 Xtended

×

A7 Xtended (Group Project)

2022

Designed in collaboration with Audi Canberra, A7 Xtended sought to augment the Audi A7 given to the University of Canberra to create new experiences, functionality, and utility for future and current users. Over the course of the unit our studio team designed and built a middle seat console to elevate the experience of the corporate car by likening the experience to that of air travel and a front window blind media system for transforming the car when not in motion to an office on the go. We exhibited our work along with the A7 after the semester. My core role in the team was design engineering and prototyping. I was one of the members responsible for realising the console prototype.
